2023 wrap up

January 13, 2024 3-minute read
2023

Here is a quick summary of what I achieved in 2023, what I’ve learned from it and my 2024 goals. Overall very happy with this year !

Successes

Personal

  • I’ve moved back to Helsinki in Feb 2023 after spending 5 months in Antwerp (Belgium). It was a fun experience, I will miss the cheap beers and the good fries ( Get drunk and filled for a tenner, unbeatable) . The Brick was a blast, joined it 3 days after I moved to Antwerp and would DEFINITELY recommend. Amazing coaches, really good vibes. They offered me the monthly price based on a 1 year subscription, because my reason to cancel it was valid : I was going to leave the country.
  • I’ve moved back to Noli Sornainen in Helsinki
  • Went to Italy and Spain, Turin is a great city and I liked Barcelona more than I was expecting. It was still so warm and sunny in October, which is amazing. But my time in a hot HCMC was a loong time ago. Being used to the Finnish weather, I don’t think I could handle a whole summer there.
  • Smoked maybe 1 or 2 cigs max ! I am glad I am done with this
  • Got a 1421 euro phone bill, but thankfully didn’t have to pay it in the end thanks to my mnager

Hard Skill - Tech

  • Definitely feels like I am better than last year
  • Started learning java for DS&A algorithm in September course 10 of the mooc,

Japanese:

  • 17 level on Wanikani

Soft Skill

  • Finished the mentoring of 6 young adults
  • Started public speaking with Toastmaster in Helsinki

Athletic Achievement (didn’t know how to call this section):

  • 9 months of crossfit : 238 sessions since Feb 2023 at Crossfit Basement Kurvi , which is literally in Noli’s Basement, making my box choice obvious despite the other options in Helsinki : No commuting, weather cannot really affect me, no excuse: just need to wake show up. My first crossfit experience was in Paris (after the lockdown) and it wasn’t that great, I’ve then tried some sessions at the Brick but again felt way behind and did other workouts instead. Coming back to Helsinki, I could have just sticked to the Noli’s Gym which is part of the amenities, but I really missed the social aspect of those workouts. I remember being so intimated when I first visited the place before joining the On Ramp at Kurvi . Since then I’ve been practicing 5 times a week. Coaches are great. Finally a sport I can mention in my resume.
  • I’ve been the fittest in my life, definitely shredded some fat
  • my lower back doesn’t hurt anymore
  • Got my strict muscle up right before my Birthday !!! I am soooo happy, have been working on this for 3 years
  • 55 kg shoulder press
  • Got knees front lever
  • Got kipping ring mu without expectation
  • Got better with Double Unders (20reps). It’s cool because I couldn’t do any at the beginning of the year.
  • Got started with running! Of course on treadmill, allowed me to Finish Ozark
